From Humble Beginnings to Trusted Leaders

Hi, I’m Hector! Founder of Apex Painting and Renovations. I believe that a fresh coat of paint can do more than just change a room—it can change how you feel about your home. I started my painting business to help homeowners in Littleton, Colorado turn their design visions into reality.

Whether it’s a bold accent wall or a complete exterior refresh, I pride myself on clean lines, honest pricing, and always showing up on time. My goal is simple: to make the process stress-free for you and to leave your space looking better than I found it. I don’t just see a house; I see a home that deserves to look its absolute best.

When I’m not behind a brush, you can usually find me in the mountains riding my bike, camping or hiking. I like working on my own DIY projects or watching the Denver Broncos play. Ready to give your space a new look? Let’s get to work!
